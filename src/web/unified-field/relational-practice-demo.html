Mutual Arrival

Œ©0: First Presence - Together

  1. Begin together with three conscious breaths
  2. Make gentle eye contact without forcing it
  3. Notice how it feels to arrive together
  4. Rest in shared presence for 1-2 minutes
  5. End by bowing to acknowledge the shared field
Duration: 3-5 minutes
Setup: Sitting face to face

You'll Know It's Working When:

  • Both people feel more settled than they did alone
  • Natural synchronization of breathing or movement
  • Sense of safety and non-judgment in shared space
  • Feeling held by something larger than individual effort

Mutual Intention Setting

Œ©1: Conscious Arrival - Together

  1. Begin with mutual Œ©0 (First Presence) practice
  2. Share: "How do I want to show up for you today?"
  3. Listen to your partner's intention without responding

  5. Set a shared intention for your time together
  6. Proceed with this mutual intention guiding you
Duration: 5-8 minutes

Mutual Sacred Witnessing

Œ©4: Sacred Listening - Together

  1. Begin with mutual Œ©0 to arrive together
  2. Person A speaks for 3 minutes while B practices sacred listening
  3. B reflects back what they heard in A's heart (not advice)
  4. Switch roles - B speaks for 3 minutes, A listens
  5. A reflects back what they heard in B's heart
  6. Share appreciation for the quality of listening received
Duration: 10-12 minutes
Prompts: "What's alive in me right now?"

Successful Sacred Listening:

  • Speaker feels deeply heard and understood
  • Listener discovers insights about the speaker's truth
  • Both people feel more connected than before
  • Natural intimacy and safety in the relationship
