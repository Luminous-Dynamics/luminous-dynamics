Your Integration Journey

A suggested path through the seven integration stars, designed for natural progression and deep embodiment.

1

Begin with Process Grace (*12)

Start by releasing perfectionism and learning to love what's incomplete. This creates spaciousness for deeper work.

2

Explore Sacred Exchange (*13)

Learn to give and receive without keeping score. This foundation supports all relationship healing.

3

Honor Your Grief (*14)

Create sacred space for loss and allow grief to transform into wisdom and compassion.

4

Embody Joy (*15)

After processing grief, learn to fully celebrate and let joy live in your body without guilt.

5

Practice Curious Questions (*16)

Transform judgment into wonder. Learn the art of sacred inquiry that opens new possibilities.

6

Dance with Your Shadow (*17)

Welcome back rejected parts of yourself. This deep integration work transforms your relating from the inside out.

7

Master Conflict Alchemy (*18)

Learn to transform discord into deeper intimacy. This culminating practice integrates all previous work.
